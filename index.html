  • About

    Fabric Media was born from the vision of true decentralization. Our strategy focuses on acquiring cornerstone art and media pieces as both a means of supporting the artists, and as a platform for sharing real wealth with the public.

    A St. Louis based company, we aim to hold these pieces with the city, and grant wealth toward municipal infrastructure repair.

    For all inquiries contact: foolishboy@fabricmedia.art
